‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about SoulStrings

How does Moodify detect my mood?

SoulStrings uses multiple AI-powered methods to detect your mood: text analysis of what you type, voice emotion recognition from your speech, and facial expression analysis through your camera. All data is processed locally for privacy.

Do I need accounts for Spotify and YouTube?

For full functionality, yes. SoulStrings requires you to connect your Spotify and/or YouTube accounts to access personalized playlists. However, you can still use mood detection features without connecting accounts.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely! We take privacy seriously. Your mood data is encrypted and stored locally. We don't share personal information with third parties. Voice and facial data are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers.

Can I use Moodify on mobile devices?

Yes! SoulStrings is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. We're also developing native mobile apps for iOS and Android to provide an even better experience on mobile.

How accurate is the mood detection?

Our AI model has been trained on thousands of samples and achieves over 85% accuracy in controlled conditions. Accuracy improves when using multiple input methods (text + voice + face). The system also learns from your feedback.
