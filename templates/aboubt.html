üéµ SoulStrings: A Mood-Based Music Recommendation System

Experience music that understands your emotions

SoulStrings is a revolutionary web application built using Flask that intelligently detects a user's mood and recommends personalized songs. It supports multiple input modalities (text, voice, and facial expression) and integrates with both Spotify and YouTube to deliver music that matches the user's current emotional state.
